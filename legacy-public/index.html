FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

What image formats are supported?

We support PNG, JPEG, JPG, and WEBP images up to 10MB in size.

How accurate is the detection?

Accuracy depends on training data quality. Our logistic regression model typically achieves 75–90% accuracy with a balanced dataset of 500+ images per class.

Are my images stored on a server?

Images are sent to the API for analysis but are not permanently stored. Scan history with thumbnails is saved locally on your device only.

Can I install this as an app?

Yes! AI or Not is a Progressive Web App. Install it from Chrome on Android or add to your home screen on iOS.

What model is used?

We use logistic regression trained on handcrafted features including color histograms, Canny edge density, Sobel texture gradients, and statistical color moments.

How accurate is the AI detection?

Our platform boasts a 99.5% accuracy rate, backed by extensive testing and validation across millions of images and videos from various AI generation models.

No, we prioritize your privacy. Files are processed in real-time and immediately deleted after analysis. We never store your uploaded content.

We support all major image formats (JPEG, PNG, GIF, WebP) and video formats (MP4, AVI, MOV, WebM) up to 50MB in size.

We offer a free tier for individual users and flexible plans for businesses. Visit our pricing page or contact sales for custom enterprise solutions.

We offer 24/7 email support, live chat during business hours, and dedicated account managers for enterprise customers. Response time is typically under 24 hours.

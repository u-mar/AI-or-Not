Our Story

Transforming Digital Authenticity

Founded in 2023, AI or Not emerged from a simple yet powerful realization: as AI-generated content becomes increasingly sophisticated, the world needs reliable tools to distinguish between artificial and authentic media.

Our founding team—comprised of AI researchers, computer vision experts, and digital ethics advocates—witnessed firsthand how rapidly advancing AI capabilities were blurring the lines between real and generated content. We knew something had to be done.

"We're not just building a detection tool; we're building trust in the digital age."

— Founding Team

Today, we serve journalists verifying sources, content creators protecting their work, researchers studying digital media, and curious individuals navigating the AI-augmented digital landscape. Our platform has analyzed over 1.2 million files across 120 countries, maintaining a 99.5% accuracy rate.
